ClipHub – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ClipHub is a macOS menu bar app for searching GIFs, pictures, videos, and vector icons, copying them to the clipboard, and recording a screen region as an animated GIF. It does not require account creation.

Data on Your Device

Settings (for example hotkey and search options), favorites, screen recordings you save, and HTTP cache data are stored locally on your Mac. The developer does not receive this data automatically.

Clipboard & Screen Recording

When you copy media or use screen recording, that processing happens on your device. The app uses the system clipboard and screen capture only as needed for features you trigger.

Network & Third-Party Services

When you search or load remote media, ClipHub connects to third-party APIs and CDNs, including for example GIPHY, Pixabay, Unsplash, and Iconify. Your search queries and standard request metadata may be processed by those providers under their own policies.

ClipHub does not operate its own backend for collecting your journal of searches or clipboard contents.

Analytics & Tracking

ClipHub does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. Technical logs may be written locally on your Mac using Apple’s logging facilities for debugging; the developer does not receive those logs unless you choose to send them.

Children’s Privacy

ClipHub is not directed at children under 13. Do not use the app if you are under the age required in your country.

Data Control & Deletion

You have full control over local data:

  • Remove favorites and recordings inside the app where supported
  • Change preferences in Settings where available
  • Delete the app to remove its local data (subject to how macOS handles app containers)
